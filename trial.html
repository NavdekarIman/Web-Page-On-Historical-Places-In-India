Historical Places in India

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. It was built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Red Fort

The Red Fort is a historic fort located in Delhi. It served as the main residence of the emperors of the Mughal dynasty for nearly 200 years.
